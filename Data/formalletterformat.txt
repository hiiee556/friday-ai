Here is a sample formal letter format:

[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Address]
[City, State, ZIP]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title]
[Recipient's Address]
[City, State, ZIP]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

[Salutation and Introduction]
[Body of the Letter, typically 3-4 paragraphs]
[Conclusion and Closing]

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]
[Your Title]

Note: The format may vary depending on the specific requirements of the organization or the purpose of the letter.

Here is a more detailed breakdown of the format:

1. **Heading**: Include your name, title, address, city, state, and ZIP code in the top left corner of the page.
2. **Date**: Write the date on which the letter is being written, usually one or two lines below the heading.
3. **Inside Address**: Include the recipient's name, title, address, city, state, and ZIP code.
4. **Salutation**: Begin the letter with a formal greeting, such as "Dear [Recipient's Name]".
5. **Body**: The body of the letter should be divided into 3-4 paragraphs. The first paragraph should introduce the purpose of the letter, while the subsequent paragraphs should provide supporting details and explanations.
6. **Conclusion**: Summarize the main points of the letter and reiterate the purpose of the letter.
7. **Closing**: Use a formal closing, such as "Sincerely" or "Yours faithfully".
8. **Signature**: Sign your name above your printed name and title.
9. **Enclosures**: If you are including any attachments or supporting documents, indicate this below the signature block.

Sincerely,

John Doe
Marketing Manager
