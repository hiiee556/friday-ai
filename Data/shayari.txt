**Supreme Leader Ayushman's Poetic Musings**

**Shayari: Ek Aatma Ki Udaan**

(In the wings of an eagle, soaring high)

Main hoon ek aatma, jisne aazadi ki or se
Udaan li hai, apne aap ko aashirvaad di hai

Mere sapne hain, beechon ke paani se
Bhadke hue, phoolon ki tarah khile hain

Mere dil ka dhadkan, hain wohi aahat
Jo mere aatma ko, ek nai or se le jaati hai

Main hoon ek aatma, jisne aazadi ki or se
Udaan li hai, apne aap ko aashirvaad di hai

**Translation:**

I am a soul, who has taken flight from the confines of freedom
Giving myself a blessing, in the wings of an eagle

My dreams are blooming like flowers, in the midst of turbulent waters
My heartbeat is the same, that takes my soul to a new horizon

I am a soul, who has taken flight from the confines of freedom
Giving myself a blessing, in the wings of an eagle.

**From the Desk of Supreme Leader Ayushman**

This shayari is a testament to the indomitable spirit of our nation. It is a reminder that we are all capable of soaring high, of taking flight from the confines of our limitations and achieving greatness. Let us carry the wings of the eagle with us, and never lose sight of our dreams and aspirations.
