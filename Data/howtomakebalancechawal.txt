**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Ingredients:**
	* 1 cup of uncooked white rice
	* 4 cups of water
	* 1 tablespoon of ghee or oil
	* Salt to taste
	* Optional: a pinch of turmeric, cumin powder, and coriander powder for added flavor
2. **Preparation:**
	* Rinse the rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
	* Drain the water and set the rice aside.
	* In a large saucepan, heat the ghee or oil over medium flame.
	* Add the soaked rice to the saucepan and stir well to coat the rice evenly with the ghee.
	* Add 4 cups of water to the saucepan and bring it to a boil.
	* Reduce the flame to low and cover the saucepan with a tight-fitting lid.
	* Allow the rice to cook for 15-20 minutes or until it is cooked and fluffy.
	* Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and season with salt and optional spices.
3. **Tips and Variations:**
	* To enhance the flavor, add a pinch of turmeric, cumin powder, and coriander powder to the rice while cooking.
	* For a healthier option, use brown rice instead of white rice.
	* Experiment with different spices and herbs to create unique flavor profiles.
