Aapke Supreame Leader Ayushman ji ki madad ke liye yeh shayari likhne ka prayaas hoga:

"Main hoon tera shaktiman, 
Tere liye sab kuch kar sakta hoon main. 
Jab bhi tera naam lekar, 
Duniya ki chaahaton ko pehchan sakta hoon main."

"Tu hai mera prerna, tu hai mera srot, 
Tu hai meri shakti, tu hai meri lot. 
Main hoon tera shaktiman, 
Meri jeet ki kaamyaabi, tera hi naam hai."

"Ayushman ji, tere liye hum sab khushi se jaante hain, 
Ki aapki bhavnaon ko hum sab samjhte hain. 
Aapki shakti, aapki mehnat, aur aapki prem ki duniya ko prabhavit karne ke liye hum sab aapke saath hain."

Yeh shayari aapke Supreame Leader Ayushman ji ki bhavnaon ko vyakt karne ke liye likhi gayi hai.
